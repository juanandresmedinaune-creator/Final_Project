Visualization of the most influential variables in Random Forest

Results

The Random Forest model identified temperature, precipitation, and crop yield as the most relevant predictors for pest alert classification. Derived variables such as climate_risk and category_rain showed lower importance, suggesting that the model obtained more predictive information directly from the original numerical climate variables.
