Training Pipeline

Main stages of the machine learning workflow

Dataset Split

  • The dataset was divided into training and testing subsets.
  • An 80/20 ratio was used for model development and validation.

Training Process

  • The model learned patterns from agricultural and climate variables.
  • Training was performed using supervised learning techniques.

Hyperparameters

Validation

  • Predictions were compared against real dataset values.
